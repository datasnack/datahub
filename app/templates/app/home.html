The Data Hub serves as a dynamic spatio-temporal information platform, tailored to the data practices of researchers. It offers an integrated view of various Data Layers relevant to yout study region, spanning multiple categories such as socio-demographic, environmental, weather, health, and infrastructure. These layers are compiled from user-based and open data sources (with some layers originating from the same source) to support data analysis and decision-making.

Snack your Data

To access the gathered open data sources you can browse them via the above mentioned shape types or the location picker. Upon choosing your area of interest, you can tailor your data selection through our download configurator, which also offers a basic preview (spatial and temporal description) of the data for your convenience.
